Case Study 1

The main objective of the park is to provide foreign and local companies with end to end infrastructural support and provide them a ready setup so that they can focus on their core business. The issues and our solution are outlined below.

Problems we solve
  • Getting land at right price
  • Developing the land
  • Cost and hassle of construction
  • Zoning, Planning Permissions & Building Codes
  • Government Approvals, Licenses & Sanctions
  • Corporate, Legal and Statutory Norms
  • Financial and Tax implications
  • Technical and Environmental Factors
Direct Benefits
  • Quick Start
  • No Debt Burden
  • Frees up capital for business
  • Financing and operational flexibility
  • Better Cash-Flow Management
  • Mitigate Business Risk
THE VALUE PROPOSITION

If a foreign company wants to set up a factory unit in Dhaka, they have to first look for land at the right location once they sort out the zoning issues. After they find the land, they have to ensure that it is a clean and non agricultural land. Then have to agree on the right price. Once they have bought the land, next step is to move on to converting the land- landfill, land development, boundary wall, etc. Once, they finally mange to do that, they are 10/12 months down. Finally they can now apply for building permissions, post sanctions, their specific business licenses and permissions0 another 6-8 months and finally after 18-20 months down they can start construction either by themselves or through third party vendors, which will take another 6-8 months. Hence, on average 2.5-3 years before they can truly start their business operations.

WE TAKE AWAY ALL THAT

So What We Offer is, Day one you come to our park, we are able to offer you ready land, it is 100% clean and legal with all governance followed and it is already converted to ready to shovel land with drainage, boundary wall, electric line (upto the plot) with building sanctions and permissions. So you are straight away able to move on to construction. Not only that we take up the construction- we invest our own capital and build it for you

BENEFITS TO MSME

For anyone starting a business you have to invest capital in land, capital in building permissions and licenses and finally on construction of the factory as well as supporting infrastructure. We take away all that! We invest the capital for you and all you do is pay the base rental on lease. Say you needed 40 crore  you can now do the same with almost 1/15th of that amount. So all that capital pool you were planning to block on land and building, you can now use for your working capital or other business activities. This makes their business much more sustainable as result of our Plug & Play offering and Pure Play Convenience- For instance walking into a brand new set office space as oppose to building the office space from scratch by yourself
