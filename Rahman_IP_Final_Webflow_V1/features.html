Location
About Gazipur

Rahman Industrial Park- Flagship, Rahman Industrial Park-Kashempur and Rahman Industrial Park-Ambagh  are all located in Gazipur, an industrial hub with access to Four Lane Highway connecting N1,N2,N3,N4 National Highway, Tongi Road and less than 24km from International Airport.

The parks are situated in Gazipur, which has evolved into a major industrial and logistics hub hosting the production plants of not only the biggest RMG brands but also FMCGs like Marico, Unilever, British American Tobacco, etc.

Connectivity
  • 3 km from Dhaka-Tangail National Highway- N4
  • 5 km from Dhaka-Chittagong National Highway- N1
  • 12 km from Dhaka-Sylhet National Highway-N2
  • 10 km from Dhaka- Mymensigh National Highway-N3
Local Advantages

  • Developed Industrial Area, Flourishing economy and one of the closest industrial hubs to the city center
  • Readily Available Skilled Pool of Workforce

  • All major banks present in the area
